Brunnergarten

If you are looking for a real alternative to the often overcrowded beer gardens and restaurants in Munich, you have come to the right place in the Brunnergarten.

The offer ranges from dishes from German cuisine to Balkan specialties.

Bamberger Haus

Our beer garden is located at the edge of the idyllic Luitpoldpark. In the shade of the trees you can make yourself comfortable - traditionally on the beer bench or stylishly in a wicker beach chair.

Children are also catered for - a great playground awaits little adventurers!
